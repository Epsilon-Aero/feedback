Our approval certifications

Epsilon Aerospace has set the benchmark in providing cabin refurbishment services to aircraft customers. We are the first facility in India to produce passenger & crew seat covers and aircraft placards with DGCA CAR 21 Subpart G certification. Our facility is in line with the European Air Safety Agency (EASA), UK CAA (Civil Aviation Authorities of United Kingdom), and India’s DGCA (Directorate General of Civil Aviation) for maintenance and production. Quality system of Epsilon Aerospace has received ISO9001:2015 accreditation from TUV SUD.
